Learning Disentanglement

 2022/12/24

Recently, I have been trying to understand the 2021 Neurips Spotlight article Self-Supervised Learning Disentangled Group Representation as Feature. While following this line of work, I found this paper really interesting. It leverages Invariant Risk Minimization (IRM) and the notion of group theory to shed light on disentangled representation, a research area that has long been investigated. However, as the paper is somehow obsecure to understand, especially for those who do not have backgound in group theory, I would like to share my perspective about this research work based on my understanding. Hopefully, we will gain new inspiration from this interesting paper together.
